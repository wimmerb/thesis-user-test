Thanks for your interest in participating in the listening test. The overall test will roughly take 15 minutes. Please take the time and carefully rate every item.

This test aims to evaluate systems meant for denoising and leakage removal
  • Denoising is the task of removing background noise (i.e. ambient noise) from a signal.

  • Leakage (or spill/bleed/acoustic echo) happens when we record ourselves while listening to a reference audio source. Under non-optimal circumstances, in our recording, the reference audio we are listening to will become audible as an artifact. Removing such artifacts is a task relevant to music and speech processing.

Instructions:
  • Most important: use high quality studio headphones!
  • On each test page, make sure to read the instructions carefully.
  • Always listen through all test files before you do any ratings to get used to the material.
  • Listen until the end and listen carefully
  • Try to rate the overall impression of a test item and don't concentrate on single aspects.
  • Sharing of sound samples used in this test is illegal. Please do not store or share them.
